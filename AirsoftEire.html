AirsoftEire is proudly 100% Irish owned and operated and was founded by Stephen Hickey & Liam Doyle in Dundalk Co Louth in 2007. We specialise in a broad range of mid to high end airsoft guns and accessories. We stock many of the more exotic brands from Japan, Taiwan and Hong Kong not typically found with other retailers. Our shipping service is the best in the industry, with same day shipping on orders placed before 1pm, Monday to Friday..
